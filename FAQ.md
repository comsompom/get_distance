# FAQ - TargetLock

## Does TargetLock require internet?
No. All processing is on-device and offline.

## What devices are supported?
Any iPhone 6s or newer running iOS 13+ (ARKit required).

## Why are my measurements inconsistent?
Tap precision, lighting, and object angle can affect results. Re-tap carefully and ensure good lighting.

## Can I measure animals?
Yes, if you know or can estimate the animal's height.

## How do I calibrate?
Use the in-app Help button for the optional manual calibration steps.
